What's Inside

Everything You Need
In One App

Real features built for real health goals — no fluff, no AI gimmicks.

Trending Recipes

Browse and discover popular, curated recipes right on the home screen. Add ingredients straight to your shopping cart with a tap.

6-Plan Meal Prep

Choose from 6 structured meal plan cards, each featuring balanced portions of rice, meat, vegetables, mallum, and salad.

Advanced BMI & Calorie Calc

Input weight, height, age, gender, activity level and goal. Get real-time BMI with recommended calories, carbs, fat and protein breakdown.

Smart Shopping Cart

Add meal ingredients directly to cart from recipes or meal plans, then locate the nearest store to pick up everything you need.

Nearby Store Locator

Find grocery stores and markets close to your location to order your cart items. Powered by real-time location services.

Smart Notifications

Stay on track with timely meal reminders and health tip notifications customised to your schedule and goals.

Getting Started

How NutriFlex
Works

Up and running in minutes — a seamless 5-step journey.

01

Personalise Your Profile

Complete a 5-step onboarding — enter your age, weight, height, fitness goals, dietary preferences and activity level.

02

Browse Trending Recipes

Explore the home feed packed with popular recipes. Tap any recipe to view full details and add it to your shopping cart.

03

Pick a Meal Plan

Select one of 6 structured meal prep plans in the Meal Prep section. Each plan includes rice, meat, two vegetables, mallum and salad.

04

Check Your BMI & Calories

Use the built-in BMI calculator with full parameters. Get a real-time BMI score and a detailed recommended macro breakdown.

05

Shop & Stay Notified

Use the store locator to find nearby shops for your cart items. Set notifications to stay consistent with your plan.

Our Story

Built by
Nutrition Navigators

NutriFlex was born from a simple belief: eating well shouldn't be complicated. We're a team of six passionate developers who combined our love for technology and health to create an app that makes balanced nutrition accessible to everyone.

From a 5-step onboarding that learns your goals, to 6 structured meal plans and a store locator that connects you to real-world ingredients — every feature is designed with purpose. No AI buzzwords. Just solid engineering, thoughtful UX, and real nutritional science.
